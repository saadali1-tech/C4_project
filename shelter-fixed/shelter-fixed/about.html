About ConnectCare Ontario

A coordination prototype for service providers supporting people experiencing or at risk of homelessness in Ontario.

What is this?

ConnectCare Ontario is a static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript prototype for frontline service providers.

When one organization cannot fully meet a client's needs, the portal helps workers find other organizations, review referral requirements, identify partnership opportunities, discuss sector trends, and locate funding resources.

Implemented Feature Areas

  1. Service Provider Directory with search by city, service, population, and keyword.
  2. Organization Profiles with services, partnerships, contact details, referral requirements, grants, and current projects.
  3. Partnership Matching that suggests organizations based on shared location, complementary services, and collaboration needs.
  4. Discussion Forum prototype for service providers to share issues, trends, and coordination ideas.
  5. Resource and Funding Library with funding opportunities, reporting requirements, guides, and templates.

Privacy Notice

This prototype does not store client data or connect to a server. All demo matching happens locally in the browser.

The discussion form stores only prototype posts in the browser's local storage so the interaction feels realistic during a demo.

Project Background

This tool was developed as a prototype concept for a Community Partnership Navigation Model.

The sample organizations, grants, forum posts, and resources are demo data for presentation and testing purposes.
